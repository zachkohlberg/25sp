CSC110 Syllabus

Class Meetings

Instructor Absence

I may hold class remotely via Zoom if I cannot attend the meeting in person (for example, if I'm sick). If I need to cancel class, then I will notify everyone as early as possible in a Blackboard announcement.

Class Structure

The course schedule will have the agenda for each class meeting, and all activities and material from class will be posted online. If you want me to cover a specific topic or spend more time on something, then let me know and I will try to fit it into the agenda for an upcoming class.

Currently, my plan is to split each week into one day of review/lecture (Mondays) and one day of in-class practice (Wednesdays). You should show up to each class prepared for what we're doing that day, which means completing any reading or other homework assignments before class starts. Showing up to a group activity unprepared is disrespectful to the people you are working with and makes it harder for the group to work on the activity.

Attendance Policy

You should attend class whenever possible, but you do not need my permission to miss class or leave early. You also don't need to explain absences to me. However, I would appreciate knowing what's going on if you have to miss multiple classes in a row or are absent frequently. The university expects me to contact your academic advisor when you have excessive or consecutive unexcused absences.

If you are feeling sick or suspect you may have COVID, the flu, or another contagious illness, then please stay home and focus on recovering. You will be okay if you miss a few classes, and you're not likely to get much out of class while you aren't feeling well. Attending class while sick also poses a risk to everyone else. If you want to attend while you suspect you have a contagious illness, then please wear a mask.
